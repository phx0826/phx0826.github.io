平衡花瓶 / Balance Vase
花瓶的造型依据清秀优雅的花器——觚，一种自宋代以后深受文人喜爱的中国传统器具。设计保留了觚上宽下窄的视觉感受，在其功能和形态上 探索更多的可能性。

插花是一种源自于东方的艺术。中国传统的插花艺术讲求因时制宜，因花制宜，在不同季节有不同的花卉，而每一种花卉独特的形态又决定着不同的花器。因此为了实现花瓶造型的可变性，花瓶分上下两部分结构，上半部分结构在不需要任何插接件的情况下可以改变角度并形成精妙的平衡，用于搭配不同种类的花卉或者插花作品。

The vase is based on a delicate gu, an elegant vase, a traditional Chinese vessel favored by scholars since the Song Dynasty. The design retains the gu's wide on top and narrow on the bottom, exploring more possibilities in its function and form.

Flower arrangement is an art from the East. The traditional Chinese art of flower arrangement emphasizes the need to adjust measures according to the time. There are different flowers in different seasons, and the unique form of each flower determines the different flower arrangement. Therefore, in order to realize the variability of the vase shape, the structure of the vase is divided into two parts, the upper part of the structure can change the Angle without any insertion pieces, which can be used to match different kinds of flowers or flower arrangement works.

材质：PP，高约300mm，尺寸可变

调味罐 / Seasoning Pot
调味罐应用了平衡系列灵活可变的结构，瓶身与瓶盖相连，用手指拨动开关即可实现瓶口的开合，便于操作和收纳。

The seasoning pot uses the balance series flexible structure, the bottle body is connected with the bottle cap, and the bottle mouth can be opened and closed by finger toggle switch, which is convenient for operation and storage.

材质：PP，高约180mm，尺寸可变
