Vintage Collection ：烛台 / 花器

本着对复古风格与古董手工艺品的热爱，我在木工坊复刻了瑞典50s的古董烛台（上左），并尝试用一种独特的材料去探索自己的风格语言（上右）。

紫光檀白皮料，又称“阴阳木”，由于硬度上的缺陷，常常作为一种紫光檀中的残次品被舍弃掉。然而正是这种拜大自然所赐的“瑕疵”，赋予了每一块阴阳木料独特的魅力。

In line with my passion for vintage style and antique handicrafts, I recreated a Vintage Swedish candlestick from the 50S (top left) in the Wood Workshop and tried to explore my own style language with a unique material (top right).

材料:黄花梨木（左），紫光檀白皮料（右）

Red light sandalwood white leather material, also known as "Yin and Yang wood", because of the hardness of the defects, often as a red light sandalwood in the defective products were abandoned. However, it is this "flaw" given by nature that gives each piece of Yin and Yang wood its unique charm
